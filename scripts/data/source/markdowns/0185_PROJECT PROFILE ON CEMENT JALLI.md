KVIC- PMEGP PROJECT PROFILE ON CEMENT JALLI Introduction : Cement Jallies have occupied an important position in the building constructions. Cement jallies are pierced panels with a a thickness of not less than 2.5 cm. Used in construction of houses, building etc. as partition panels in the walls and ventilators. Manufacturing method:- Raw materials like cement, sand, stone powder is to be mixed with the adding water and poured the slurry into the wooden or iron frames having inside the ironrods.
